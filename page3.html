How To Become A Great Hockey Player

OFF ICE TRAINNING

Off ice training can help you with your game play on the ice. Working out off ice training can keep you in shape and developpe your hockey game. In experience I can tell you that off ice training is as important as on ice training. Before games and practices I would always warm-up my hands by doing some stick handeling. As I hockey player I have lots of experience and know what I am talking about.

WATCH THE PRO'S

Learning and watchig the proffesional Hockey players in the NHL or any hig level league can help you with your hockey career. By watching them its like getting some advice from them. There is so much to learn from them. By watching their teachniques, passing, skating and shooting will improve your game play and make you a better hockey player if you follow their methods.

ON ICE TRAINNING

On the ice you can developpe your skills and train for games. Practices help gain knoledge about Hockey and help practice things you want to improve on.On ice training will help you with real in game situations and will developpe your hockey IQ.
