The Playoffs

The Playoff format is different in very sport. With hockey it is not a single knock-out. Each round is composed of 7 games and the first team that wins 4 games first moves on. The playoffs are played at the end of each NHL season when each team has played 82 games. The playoffs were made to determine the champion of each season by moving on in the brackets and then winning the Finals. Once yo win the Playoffs Finals, the team is awarded The Stanley Cup, the greatest award in the game of hockey.

How it Works

In the NHL there are 32 teams with 4 Divisions containing 8 teams each. The Divisons on the Eastern Conference side is the Metropolitan and Atlantic and the Western side composes of the Pacific and Central. If you have ever seen a NHL playoff bracket then you will know that only 16 out of the 32 teams with enter. The 3 leaders with the most points in each division will make the playoffs. As you can see the red boxes indicate that you hav a spot in the playoffs. In the Atlantic division, the Florida Panthers, Tampa Bay Lightning and Detroit Red Wings made the playoffs. In the Metropolitan division the Washingtn Capitals, Pittsburgh Penguins and New York Rangers move on. In the Pacific Division the Anaheim Duck, Los Angeles Kings and San Jose Sharks got in. Finally in the Central Division the Dallas Stars, St. Louis Blues and Chicago Blackhawks got in.

The WILD CARDS

So far 12 teams have entered the Stanley Cup Playoffs and their are 16 teams that make it. For the other 4 teams that enter are decided in the Wild card standings. The top 2 teams in the Wild card in each conference will take the remaining 4 spots in the playoffs. In the Eastern Conference side in the Wild card the New York Islanders and the Philidelphia Flyers are 2 reamaining teams who will extend their season in the playoffs. In the Western Conference side in the Wild card the Nashville Predators and the Minnesota Wild will also extend their season in the playoffs.

WHO PLAYS WHO

To figure out who play who in the first round of the Stanley Cup Playoffs is to check the standigs. In the first round, the top team in the conference plays the second wild-card team of their conference. The other division winner takes on the other wild-card team in their division. Team 2 in their division will play againsts team 3 from the same division.

How To Win

To win the Stanley Cup Playoffs you must move on from the First round to the Second round to the Semi Finals and then finally The Stanley Cup Finals. In order to move on you must defeat your opponents 4 times within the 7 game series. Once you win 4 games against your opponent you then move on to the next round and your opponent is illiminated. As you move on to each round 2 teams with then face each other in the Staney Cup Finals to fight for the possesion of the Stanley Cup.
